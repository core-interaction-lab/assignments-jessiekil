Project


For Interaction Studio class' second project, we were assigned to choose a text with a minimum of 2000 words. I chose a New York Times article that I saw back in 2021. This article is called, "Let's Meet Again in 5 Years." I chose this article because I am also in a long distance relationship and I was able to relate deeply. I wanted this project to serve as an opportunity to look at long distance in a more positive and 'interactive' way.


In order to this, I researched the correct color palettes, fonts, and interactive elements for me and the users. I created different mockups on Figma and led to this website. For this project, I wanted my main symbol to be a pumping heart.


I categorized my texts in 4 different stages: Resting, Maximum Target, Moderate Intensity, and High Intensity. I categorized the texts into these four categories, use a slider to move around the different stages of texts. While they are using the slider, I wanted the heart to pump according to the heartbeat stage.


For instance, the category High Intensity, would be the fastest beating heart out of all the stages. Whereas, resting would be the slowest beating heart.
